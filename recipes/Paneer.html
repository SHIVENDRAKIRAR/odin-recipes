Matar Paneer: A Classic Indian Curry

Matar Paneer is a delicious and comforting North Indian curry made with tender green peas (matar) and soft paneer cubes simmered in a rich, spiced tomato and onion gravy. This vegetarian dish is a staple in Indian households and a favorite at special occasions and festivals. Packed with protein and nutrients, Matar Paneer is not only satisfying but also a healthy choice. It pairs wonderfully with naan, roti, or steamed rice, making it a versatile and loved dish across all age groups.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 medium onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup cream or milk (optional, for richness)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds; let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies; cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add green peas and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add water (1-2 cups) to adjust the gravy consistency and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Gently add paneer cubes and mix well; simmer for another 5 minutes.
  8. Stir in garam masala, crushed kasuri methi, and cream or milk (if using) for a rich flavor.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Serve the creamy and flavorful Matar Paneer with naan, roti, or steamed rice for a delightful meal!
